Access to Public Printers

If you need to print but there are no printers accessible in your lab, we have four locations across multiple floors in the Iribe Center with publicly accessible printers.

Public Printers in room 3208

Color Printer: cps432-3208 HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M653
Black/White Printer: ps432-3208 HP LaserJet Enterprise Flow MFP M633

Public Printers in room 4149

Color Printer: cps432-4149 HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M653
Black/White Printer: ps432-4149 HP LaserJet Enterprise Flow MFP M633

Public Printers in room 4208

Color Printer: cps432-4208 HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M653
Black/White Printer: ps432-4208 HP LaserJet Enterprise Flow MFP M633

  • ps432-4208 also has faxing capability, outlined here
Public Printers in room 5208

Color Printer: cps432-5208 HP Color LaserJet Enterprise M653
Black/White Printer: ps432-5208 HP LaserJet Enterprise Flow MFP M633
